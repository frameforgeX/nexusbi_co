Senior Data Analyst

Full-time
Hybrid (3 days in-office)

We're looking for an experienced Data Analyst to join our team and help clients extract meaningful insights from their data. In this role, you'll work closely with our consulting team to develop data models, create visualizations, and provide data-driven recommendations to clients.

  • Design and develop reports and dashboards using tools like Power BI, Tableau, or similar
  • Extract, transform, and load data from various sources
  • Identify trends and patterns in complex datasets
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements
  • Present findings to stakeholders and provide actionable recommendations
  • Implement data quality and governance procedures

  • Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field
  • 3+ years experience in data analysis and business intelligence
  • Proficiency in SQL and Excel
  • Experience with visualization tools like Power BI or Tableau
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
